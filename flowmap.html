Sophia Musoki Geovisualization Student

This flow map reveals that Agentina, Chile and Uruguay are the top importers of bananas while Ecuador is the largest exporter of bananas. This can be seen with how the flow arrows that represent imports are coming from Ecuador and Bolivia to Argentina, Chile and Uruguay. There is also a reverse flow where Chile exports to Peru and Argentina to Paraguay. This could possibly be that the largest importer, Argentina, has the resources and infrastructure to export to neighboring countries. That the biggest exporter is Ecuador is justified given its geographical location and the corresponding tropical climate.
