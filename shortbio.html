Sophia Musoki Geovisualization Student

Sophia is a student in the course GEOG 3540: Geographic Visualization. Creating these geovisualization projects throughout the semester has been a learning experience. Her goal was to learn the skills that would help her visualize her thesis project titled: ‘Unpacking Rural Women Farmers' Knowledge of Indigenous Wild and Leafy Green Vegetables in Western Uganda from a Decolonial Perspective’. The study will be conducted in Kasese District, Uganda. Through this study, she will explore, how food knowledge is produced and passed on/ preserved by rural women subsistence farmers. She hopes you enjoy browsing through the projects in her Portfolio. If there is a project that resonated the most with you, do not hesitate to send her an email about it.
